Triple chocolate chunk cookies recipe

How to make a Triple-chocolate-chunk-cookie

Step by step

  1. Prep 15 minutes

  2. Cook 10 minutes

  3. Additional1 hour

  4. Total 1 hour 25 minutes

  5. Servings 12

  6. Yield 12 cookies

Ingredients

  • ½ cup unsalted butter, at room temperature

  • ½ cup white sugar

  • 1 teaspoon salt

  • ¾ cup brown sugar

  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

  • 1 ½ cups all-purpose flour

  • ¾ teaspoon baking soda

  • 1 cup milk chocolate chips

  • 1 (3 ounce) bar dark chocolate, cut into chunks

  • 3 tablespoons white chocolate chips

  • 1 large egg
